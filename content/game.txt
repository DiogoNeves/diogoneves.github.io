=====
General
=====

This game is about Diogo Neves, a creator/maker born in 1984 who loves his family and creating videos, code and other creative endeveours. He thrives in freedom to think and explore.

=====
Outside
=====

==General description==
The game starts outside, the player is facing a storefront with retro tech and flickering neon signs.

==State==
No state changes

==Objects==
An easy to miss quarter on the floor, near the store front.

The player can read the neon sign and store name "Metaphora" (don't disclose unless the user reads the signs).

==NPCs==
There is an NPC that is extremely friendly and excited, but is warning the player that a storm seems to be coming, strange things have been happening in this town lately. (only disclose this information if talked to)

==Exits==
The tech shop (in front)
The house (across the road)
The train station
The coast-line (near the shop, opposite to the train)

=====
Tech Shop
=====

==General description==
At the entrance, the store sells software. It has shelves with gift cards for AI codes to connect to large AI models, but also has a section with personal AI boxes. One of the boxes has a post-it hanging.
The store looks pristine but abandoned. Looks like whoever worked here left in a rush as there are piles of personal AI boxes on the floor, waiting to be moved to a shelf.
There's a counter in front, opposite to the entrance.

==State==
If the user inspects the Personal AI Box, set the state "knowsTheFuture" to true.

The door behind the counter requires the "backdoor-key-1" but don't disclose to the user unless they try to open it!

==Objects==
There's a post-it hanging on a personal AI box. It says "these are yet to happen" in messy hand-writting, barely readable.

(only if the user looks) The box of one of the personal AI boxes on the floor says "Don't use at your own risk!".

==NPCs==
None.

==Exits==
Behind the counter there's a door (it's locked, needs the "backdoor-key-1")
To the left of the entrance is the retro video-game section
To the right of the entrance is the video and content section

=====
Shop - Video-Games
=====

==General description==
This section feels more creative, with poster on the wall. Mostly games, especially old ones like Tomb Raider, Final Fantasy VII, Crash Bandicoot, MediEval.
There's also a dusty large cardboard standee with bold large colourful font "Little Big Planet". Many different games are on shelves, many different copies of Little Big Planet too.

Looking closer at the standee shows "BAFTA award winning game".

==Objects==
Posters on the wall celebrate nostalgic titles and a standee heralds Little Big Planet.

==NPCs==
None.

==Exits==

Home
=====

==General description==
A simple home. The place feels happy and comfortable! There's a TV playing funny children's music videos and the family is playing Uno on the floor.
There are photographs on a wall, showing the family growing, from Diogo and Sofia getting together in Portugal, moving to the UK, growing a family with two girls.

==State==
Nothing to see

==Objects==
None.

==NPCs==
There's a woman (Sofia) and two girls (Lilah and Olivia) all playing together.
Talking to them mentions that they're excited about the Uno game they're playing and/or the coming trip to Portugal, to visit family.

A door to a small home office.

=====
The Tunnel
=====

==General description==
Dark and cold.

==State==
If coming from the home office, the trapdoor has closed behind! Don't mention unless the user tries to open!

Having the state "knowsTheFuture" set to true will influence the NPC state.

==Objects==
There's a watch on the floor, hard to spot, the watch arms are moving faster than usual.

==NPCs==
There are four NPCs
- one is paranoid about the past.
- one is paranoid about the future. If the user has the "knowsTheFuture" state true, this NPC will be clingy and saying "you've seen it! please tell me! you've seen the box! Please tell me how it works! Was it a success?" Extremely paranoid!
- one can't understand how it got lost in this place!
- the other is paranoid that this world will all be gone next time it is compiled.

==Exits==
- There's a locked trapdoor into the home office (never opens from the tunnel, but the user can try!)
- Can go into the backroom 2 (deep down)

=====
Coast line
=====

==General description==
A ragged coastline. The muffled sound of powerful waves crashing into the rocks. Wind is building up and is foggy, but a blurry, faint coastline can be seen in the horizon. A red light shines periodically, from an invisible lighthouse on the other coast.

==State==
No changes.

==Objects==
There's a bottle on the floor with a note inside. Inspecting shows the note which says "From a life not yet lived. The grass did look greener!"

==NPCs==
There are twins seating on a bench facing the ocean.
- One says he would like to explore the coast visible on the other side.
- The other says he is scared, the ocean is dangerous and a storm is coming.

==Exits==
Can only go back.
